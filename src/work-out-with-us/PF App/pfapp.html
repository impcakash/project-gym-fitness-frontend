    Mobile App FAQs

    Where can I locate the barcode to link my club membership to the PF App?
    Your barcode can be found on your physical membership card or in your welcome email.
    Can I use my planetfitness.com login credentials to log in to the app?
    Yes, you can use the same login credentials from planetfitness.com to access the mobile app.
    I did not receive the verification email. What should I do?
    Please check your spam folder. If you still can't find it, you can request a new verification email from the app.
